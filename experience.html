School Coordinator

Company: Mai Valley Boarding School

Duration: April 2020 - June 2024

Description: As a School Coordinator, I ensured seamless coordination between key stakeholders—including students, teachers, administration, guardians, staff, and external partners—to maintain an efficient and well-structured learning environment.

  • Academic Management: Designed and scheduled curricula, developed class routines, managed examinations, and oversaw the publication of results.
  • Event Supervision: Organized and supervised school events, ensuring effective planning and participation.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Conducted meetings with teachers and guardians, fostering collaboration and transparent communication.
  • Conflict Resolution: Handled concerns and complaints, implementing solutions to sustain a positive school atmosphere.
  • Administrative Coordination: Facilitated communication between different departments, aligning operations with institutional goals.
  • Resource Management: Oversaw the allocation of educational materials, classroom logistics, and event resources.
  • Leadership & Mentorship: Provided guidance to students and teachers, promoting academic success and holistic development.
  • Policy Implementation: Executed school policies, regulations, and disciplinary measures to maintain institutional standards.

This role helped me develop strong leadership, problem-solving, and organizational skills while contributing to a dynamic educational environment.
